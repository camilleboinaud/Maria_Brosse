    Maria Brosse & Co.

    Maria always dreamed to become a plumber. Help her and her friends to realize this dream by finding your way in the pipe labyrinth to end levels with the shortest time !

    Presentation

    This game is part of the DeViNT 2014's project. It was developed for the young partially-sighted persons form L'école du chateau (Nice, France) and the institute Clément Ader (Nice, France). It was developed by three students from the french engineering school Polytech Nice-Sophia in 2014 :

    • Bénédicte Lagouge
    • Maxime Multari
    • Camille Boinaud

    More details on our project website.
